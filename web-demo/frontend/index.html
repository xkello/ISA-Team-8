Risk Assessment

We are the developers of said system as we are

a natural or legal person, public authority, agency or other body that develops an AI system or a general purpose AI model (or that has an AI system or a general purpose AI model developed) and places them on the market or puts the system into service under its own name or trademark, whether for payment or free of charge;

We do not fit other legal definitions such as deployer, distributor, importer, authorised representative or product manufacturer.

GPAI

GPAI model means an AI model, including when trained with a large amount of data using self-supervision at scale, that displays significant generality and is capable to competently perform a wide range of distinct tasks regardless of the way the model is placed on the market and that can be integrated into a variety of downstream systems or applications. This does not cover AI models that are used before release on the market for research, development and prototyping activities.
GPAI system means an AI system which is based on a general purpose AI model, that has the capability to serve a variety of purposes, both for direct use as well as for integration in other AI systems.

Based on this, our model is not General purpose AI (GPAI). Our models only serve a specific, narrow purpose (restaurant recommendation) and do not display generality. Our model cannot be integrated with other systems in the way, for instance LLMs, can.

The EU AI Act prohibits manipulative AI. As a AI Recommendation System can be manipulative (especially when it is untransparent and biased), we can undertake these measures to decrease manipulativeness:

  • offer clear controls (like our restaurant filters)
  • explain why a recommendation was made (based on user's history)
  • provide an opportunity to delete some visits from history or even reset the history as a whole, thus allowing for greater control over user's privacy
  • new, yet unestablished restaurants can be boosted in recommendations, thus increasing fairness

We conducted our assessment based on this high-level summary of the EU AI Act.
